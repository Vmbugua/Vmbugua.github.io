About

A little about me

I'm a data science student at Moringa School in Nairobi, currently building out the core skills of a data analyst — Python, Excel, SQL, pandas, and data visualization. I'm based between Kiambu and Nairobi, and most days you'll find me working through a dataset, a notebook, or a new SQL query, piecing together the technical foundation for the work I want to do long-term.

My interest started in high school, working with Excel — I kept practicing its features until I stumbled into analysis, and taught myself from there. What excites me most are problems with real, tangible stakes, not abstract ones.

What drives me is being able to help small companies grow by using data to make decisions, and to build tracking records that make it easier to look into past performance and plan for the future.
